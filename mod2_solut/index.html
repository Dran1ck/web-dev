Our Menu

Chicken

Savoury chicken. Four legs and four thighs. Please note that the white meat substitution is only available for regular fried chicken.

Beef

Silverside and topside are leanear cuts of meat, well-suited to slow-cooking. The stock and juices make a great sauce when combined with caramelised onions.

Pork

After a slow braise, pork shoulder pieces are drizzled with some of the fat in the cooking liquid, and baked until browned and crisp.
